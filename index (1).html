How OTR Works
Full methodology →
01
We capture every take
Podcasts, radio, TV, columns, social media — every public prediction from tracked personalities is ingested, transcribed, and classified automatically.
02
Every take gets a Line
The OTR Line is calculated from four factors: how bold the call is, how specific the language is, the time horizon, and the category. Bolder, more specific calls carry bigger Lines.
03
Outcomes are verified
When results come in, takes are verified against live sports data. Correct calls bank the full Line. Wrong calls lose it. No partial credit. No running from it.
04
Scores are permanent
The OTR Score is a cumulative running total. It goes up and down as takes resolve. Deleted tweets are archived. Retroactive reframes are flagged. Nothing disappears.
