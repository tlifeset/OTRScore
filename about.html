About OTR
On The
Record

Welcome to OTR Score — the ultimate destination for sports fans who are just as obsessed with the hot takes as we are with the games themselves. We're a team of die-hard fans who spend our days (and nights) listening to every podcast, watching every show, and scrolling through social media to track what the biggest names in sports media are actually saying.

This isn't just final scores and over/unders. We're talking about the deep takes — which draft picks end up being busts, which players crack the top 5 at their position, which franchises are headed for a dynasty or a decade of irrelevance. Every call, logged and scored.

We built OTR Score because in this new era of sports gambling and hot take culture, it's finally time to hold these so-called experts accountable. Our proprietary scoring system factors in how bold the call was, how specific the language was, and how long they were willing to stand behind it. No more running from bad takes. No more convenient memory loss. Everything is on the record — permanently.

How we score

Every take is assigned an OTR Line based on how bold, specific, and long-horizon the call is. Correct takes bank the full Line. Wrong takes lose it. Sportsbook bets made publicly are scored the same way — with difficulty set automatically by the posted odds.
